PAVING

We use a number of different types of Paving stones to suit any customers vision.

  • Indian Stone

    Indian paving stone are generally imporrted from India and are known for being natrually beautiful viened stone.

  • Raj Green

    Raj Green Sandstone is a riven Sandstone with subtle greens, greys and browns for Traditional looking patios. The colours are only a reference and because of the nature of natural stone, there may be slight colour variation with tones and patterns.

  • Candle Grey

    Kandla Grey is the most popular sandstone patio slab on the UK market. It's subtle blend of greys are the perfect accent for any project without #overwhelming the space with colour.

  • Buff

    Buff Paving Slabs will brighten up any dark areas within your patio to create an added sense of space to your garden! Available in a range of options, buff paving is a very popular choice for any style and is available in Sandstone or Porcelain to transform your exterior into the patio you've always dreamed of!

  • Limestone

    Limestone is a smooth and flat paving stone. This makes it an ideal choice for driveways, paths and patios. However, it also works well in covered outdoor areas such as courtyards. Limestone paving absorbs a minimal amount of water during rainfall.

Both our concrete and porceln pvaing flags come in a wide range of sizes and shapes to create uniform and a more natural layout.
