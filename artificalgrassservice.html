ARTIFICAL GRASS

We supply high quality artifical grass with the most popular being 40mm super soft, but we can supply any sizes if you have a preference. Our team fit the artifical grass to a high level of quality.

  • Artifical grass has a wonderful asthetic all the year round as unlike real grass it does not change based on the weather.

  • There is much less maintenance required compared to grass as it does not need to be watered, mown or fertilised so you can enjoy the beautiful aspect of grass wihtout the maintenance of real grass.

  • Artifical grass is pet-friendly as it cannot be dug up by pets so there is no mud patches or damage by per urine which can be easily cleaned from artifical grass using light detergetn and water with products avilable specially to suit artifical grass.

  • Artifical grass can be chosen to a customers preference which a variety of length, texcture, density and even colour available.

  • It is perfect for areas which get little to no sun as artifcal grass does not require sunlight so unlike real grass will not turn brown due to lack of sunlight.

  • It can also be used in a large array of areas and uses not just the garden such as play areas, gyms, schools, hotels and many more....
